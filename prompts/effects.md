## SECOND ORDER ANALYSIS

Second Order Effects Methods:
1. keep asking "And then what?"
2. Instead of asking how to achieve a positive goal, ask how to achieve the opposite negative outcome, and then avoid those steps. This naturally reveals second-order risks that are not obvious when thinking forward
3. Two track thinking
   - Rational Factors
   - Psychological Factors
4. Find reinforcement loops or balancing loops
5. What is the 10-month and 10-year outcome?
6. Based on the stakeholders, create a map of all the secondary stakeholders affected because of these stakeholders
