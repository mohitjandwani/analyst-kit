Investigate whether public search interest leads Roblox (RBLX) business results.

Gather three series covering the last 3 full years:

1. Google Trends search interest for the term "Roblox" (worldwide). Trends data is
   weekly or monthly — aggregate it to calendar quarters (mean interest per quarter).
2. Quarterly bookings (a non-GAAP KPI from Roblox's earnings releases).
3. Quarterly GAAP revenue.

Then compare them on a like-for-like basis: search interest is an index while bookings
and revenue are dollars, so compare year-over-year growth rates of all three series, not
raw levels. Produce a single report containing one chart of the three YoY growth series
on a shared quarterly time axis, a short table of the underlying quarterly values, and a
2-3 paragraph conclusion on whether search interest moved before, with, or after
bookings — including any quarter where they clearly diverged. Cite the source of each
series.

Assemble the final deliverable with the reporting skill: a branded PDF report written to `output/10-rblx-trends-bookings.pdf`.
